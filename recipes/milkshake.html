Mint Chocolate Dairy-Free Milkshake


Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 3 scoops dairy-free vanilla ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup mint leaf(10 g)
  • ⅔ cup dairy-free milk(160 g)
  • ¼ cup ice cube(50 g)

Recipe

  1. In a blender, combine frozen bananas, dairy-free ice cream, cocoa powder, fresh mint, dairy-free milk, and ice cubes.
  2. Blend until combined.
